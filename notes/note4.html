Note 4: ASCE41-23 Tier 3 Assessment

Detailed Evaluation

Required Construction Documentation

  • Architectural drawings
  • Structural as-built drawings (for the in-situ building)
  • Geotechnical report

Additional Information (May Be Provided)

  • Structural design report
  • Digital structural model (ETABS or equivalent)
  • Inspection/material testing reports
  • Building maintenance records
  • MEP construction documentation

Activities Required

  • Site visit
  • Non-invasive concrete testing
  • Concrete cores and reinforcement bars scanning may be required on a case by case basis
