Note 2: Peer Review

For New Leases or Lease Renewal

Required Construction Documentation

  • Structural design report - Produced by Engineer of Record (EoR) outlining building layout, gravity/lateral systems, design parameters
  • Structural as-built drawings (for the in-situ building)
  • Digital structural model (ETABS or equivalent) including geometry, material properties, loading assumptions and boundary conditions
  • Architectural drawings

Additional Information (May Be Provided)

  • Inspection/material testing reports
  • Building maintenance records
  • Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) construction documentation
  • Geotechnical report
